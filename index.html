Seasonal Game

Click on the seasonal images below!

The goal of the game is to match the randomly generated 'Target Score' by clicking on the seasonal pictures. Each picture is assigned a random number, and it's your goal to click on the seasonal pictures and add up your Current Score to match the Target Score. If your score goes over the Target Score, you lose the game.

When you win, your win total will increase and the game will reset with a new Target Score. If you lose, your losses will increase, the game will reset, and a new Target Score will be assigned.
